Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe with Taskly? +

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all your data. This means only you can access your tasks and notes. We cannot see your data, and no one else can either.

Can I use Taskly with my team? +

Yes! Taskly is built for collaboration. You can create shared projects, assign tasks to team members, leave comments, and see real-time updates to keep everyone in sync.

What platforms does Taskly support? +

Taskly will be available on all major platforms, including Web, iOS, Android, macOS, and Windows. Your data syncs seamlessly across all of them.

When will Taskly be available? +
